  future earnings and adjusts projections accordingly. In contrast, **loan book-based valuations tend
  to overstate the economic value of the loan portfolio** without incorporating the full extent of
  credit risk.

  requirements**, **industry standards**, and the **specific assumptions being made** during the DCF
